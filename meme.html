Meme Mashup Art

Grump Old Dictators

Starting with the scandals that plague the current sitting president of the United States, I blended the pee tape, Stormy Daniels affair, collusion with Russia, and Putin as Trump’s puppet master together into the movie poster from Grumpy Old Men. I then used images of Trump, Putin, and Stormy Daniels, to morph the characters from the frequently joked about movie about two old guys that don’t seem to get along. The statement I’m attempting to make is very much a political one, which uses the “comedy” of one of Trump’s facial expressions coupled with a capture from video of Putin at the US/Russia Summit 2018, along with a porn star and a reference to the supposed pee-tape.
